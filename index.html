Salt Lake City Roleplay is a community ERLC server based on Emergency Response: Liberty County where players can take part in realistic roleplay scenarios.


The server is inspired by a Salt Lake City-style setting and allows members to choose different roles like civilian, public safety, fire/rescue, or dispatch.


Our goal is to keep roleplay fun, organized, and as realistic as possible. We use an external community server for communication, updates, and applications to keep everything running smoothly.


Members are expected to stay in character, follow the rules, and create fair and detailed scenarios. Whether you're responding to calls or just driving around as a civilian, the focus is on making the experience enjoyable for everyone.


Overall, Salt Lake City Roleplay is built around a strong community, active roleplay, and a balanced environment that isn’t overly strict but still maintains quality.
